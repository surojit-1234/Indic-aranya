About Us

Indic Aranya Foundation is a non-profit organization founded in 2024 to promote sustainable activities by encouraging and engaging youths.

Mission: Conserving and sustaining biodiversity of all biogeographic zones, ecosystems, states and UTs, protected areas, landscapes, sacred groups and pollution abetment through research, education, participation and awareness.

Vision: To create a healthy planet through broad based basic and advanced research.

Join us in to create a better future for generations to come.
